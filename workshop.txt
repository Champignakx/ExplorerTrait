description=This mod adds an Explorer trait with the following effects:
description=[list]
description=[*] Costs 1 points,
description=[*] Doubles the drawing map radius.(now configurable in sandbox settings, default = 90)
description=[*] Minor foraging stats: +0.5 search radius, -10% weather effect, -2% darkness effect, and +3% berries, mushrooms, forest rarities, wild plants, wild herbs and firewood.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h1]Dynamic Update (27/12/2022)[/h1]
description=If you check the dynamic option, you'll be able to acquire the Explorer trait dynamically.
description=If you check the option to show the number of cells explored, it will keep track of explored cells, even after you aquire the trait.
description=[i]All thanks and credits to @MusicManiac for the Dynamic update.[/i]
description=
description=[u]Compatibility:[/u]
description=If you have a mod that changes the info screen, (like Reorganized Info Screen) you'll need to uncheck the option to show the explored cells on the infor screen in sandbox settings. If it is checked, it will keep the vanilla info screen with the cells explored stat.
description=
